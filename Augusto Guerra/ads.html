Algorithms and data structures


algorithms are not necessarily related to software development,however,nowadays when it comes to algorithms we are usually talking about something related to development.

They consist of step-by-step instructions that process an input of information and deliver an output.

The field of study of algorithms is quite broad and we can analyze their efficiency based on execution time, memory expenditure when implemented and among other factors.

A data structure is a collection of information and data connections, it can be simple like linked lists or more complex things like trees.
